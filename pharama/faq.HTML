Frequently Asked Questions

What Types of Medical Laboratory Tests Do You Offer?
We offer blood tests, urine tests, thyroid tests, diabetes screening, full body checkups, and many specialized diagnostic services.
How Will I Receive My Lab Test Results?
Results are available via email, SMS, or can be downloaded from our secure online portal.
Is a Doctor’s Referral or Prescription Required?
Most routine tests do not require a referral, but some specialized tests may require a prescription.
Are Your Laboratory Services Certified and Regulated?
Yes, our laboratory follows certified medical standards and strict quality control procedures.
How Can I Schedule a Test Appointment?
You can schedule online through our website or contact our support team.
What Health and Safety Measures Are in Place?
We maintain strict hygiene, sterilized equipment, and trained staff to ensure patient safety.
What Is the Expected Turnaround Time for Results?
Most routine results are available within 24–48 hours.
Can I Customize My Own Test Panel?
Yes, you can customize your test panel according to your health needs.
